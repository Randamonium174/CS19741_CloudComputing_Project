Common Issues & Solutions

  • Visitor QR code not recognized: Verify the QR code belongs to the correct visitor ID. Staff can manually search by visitor name or ID.
  • Walk-in visitor unable to generate pass: Ensure all required fields are completed and a valid photo is uploaded before generating the pass.
  • Pre-registered visitor forgot QR code: Staff can retrieve the visitor’s pass from the system using their registration details or phone number.
  • Pass validity expired: Check the “Valid Until” date and time on the pass. Visitors must renew their registration if expired.
  • Photo upload issues: Only JPG/PNG formats are supported. If the upload fails, try a smaller image or different browser.
